The Best Blueberry Muffins

What's better than a blueberry muffin to start(or end)your day? I've always loved the blueberry muffins from Panera but after tweaking this recipe a bit, these are pretty dang close(if not better). They are so moist and have a deliciouse crumbly streussel on top. Hope you enjoy!

Ingredients

This recipe makes 12 muffins

  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 TBS vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup sour cream
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tspn baking power
  • 1/4 tspn salt
  • 2 cups blueberries

Instructions

  • Mix butter and sugar
  • Add in one egg and then add vanilla, sour cream, and milk. Mix again, Set aside.
  • Gently fold the dry batter into the wet m batter
  • Fold in blueberries and scoop mixture into baking pan
  • Bake for 25-30 minutes at 400
